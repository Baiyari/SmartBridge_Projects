Built for Indian Banking Compliance

1. Ingest

Connects securely to your internal policy repositories (SharePoint, Confluence) while continuously monitoring RBI and SEBI portals for new circulars and master directions.

2. Extract

Our proprietary LLM pipeline breaks down dense regulatory text into discrete, enforceable obligations, identifying deadlines, conditions, and penal clauses.

3. Compare

Using semantic vector search, RegIntel maps new regulatory obligations against your existing SOPs and policies to pinpoint exact discrepancies.

4. Score

Quantifies compliance risk exposure, assigning severity bands and generating actionable remediation reports for your audit committees.
